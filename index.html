// solution programs
6 Evidence-Based Interventions
Each program is triggered by a specific KPI threshold. This is the actual solution — not just monitoring, but acting on data.
💡
Solution Logic: The dashboard identifies who has a problem and how severe it is. Each intervention is activated by specific data thresholds — making this evidence-based governance, not guesswork.
01
🏫
AI Literacy Program
TRIGGERED BY: Verification Freq = 1.1/10 · 485 youth verify 0×
School curriculum teaching youth when to use AI, when to verify, and when to think independently. Weekly fact-check challenges where students cross-reference AI outputs before acting.
🔴 URGENT — START NOW
02
🧩
AI-Free Decision Zones
TRIGGERED BY: 32.4% cannot decide w/o AI · Delay = 85 sec avg
Designated class periods where AI tools are disabled. Youth solve problems independently within time limits. Targets the 486 youth with confirmed cognitive dependency — rebuilds autonomous thinking.
🔴 URGENT — START NOW
03
⏱️
Screen Time Guidelines
TRIGGERED BY: 30% stiffness · 23% eye strain · 118 min/day avg
City-recommended 90-minute daily cap for AI-focused screen time for youth under 18. Backed directly by scatter evidence: youth exceeding 200 min/day show 4× higher physical discomfort.
🟡 MEDIUM PRIORITY
04
👨‍⚕️
Counselor Alert System
TRIGGERED BY: 285 youth at High reliance level (4–5)
Dashboard generates monthly risk reports for school counselors, flagging youth with high reliance + low verification + physical discomfort for 1-on-1 digital wellness check-ins.
🟡 MEDIUM PRIORITY
05
📢
Public Awareness Campaign
TRIGGERED BY: 64.8% AI-assisted decisions · City expansion plans
City-wide campaign for parents and youth. Goal: reduce AI-assisted decision rate from 64.8% to below 50% in 12 months while maintaining beneficial AI use in education.
🔵 LONG-TERM
06
📈
Monthly Progress Tracking
TRIGGERED BY: All KPIs — ongoing monitoring required
Dashboard re-measured monthly to track intervention impact. City leadership sees if verification rates improve, reliance decreases, and wellness recovers — closing the evidence loop for responsible AI expansion.
